FEEL THE FIT CURIOSITY

DRINK PLENTY OF WATER


Drinking 2 cups before a meal can cut calories intake by 15% and keep skin glowing.

EAT ONE LESS BITE


Cut 250 calories from your food and burn 250 calories with exercise each day and you can lose 3500 calories,or 1 pound a week.

MOVE THAT BODY


30 minutes of walking can burn 200-250 calories.

SLEEP


It keeps you well because chemicals important to your immune system are secreted during sleep.

START OFF SLOW!


Starting a new exercise routine can be challenging.The goal is to start slowly, build up your fitness level, and let your body rest from time to time to help prevent injuries.

PUMP YOURSELF UP!


Positive self-talk can boost your motivation. Look in the mirror and observe how strong your muscles are. Applaud yourself for getting fit. Recognize the goals you've met.

MOTIVATING MUSIC!


Music can help take your workout up a notch if it’s music that appeals to you. It’s like having a trainer right in your ears.

STRETCH!


Don't forget to stretch after exercise. If you can loosen your muscles post-workout, you’ll have happy joints ready to go for your next sweat session and less pain.
